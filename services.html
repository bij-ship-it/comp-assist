Compensation Assistance Services is one of Australia's leading and most successful vocational and functional providers. We deliver comprehensive, multi-disciplinary assessments that support compensation claims, return-to-work decisions, and workplace safety initiatives.

Our team includes expert witness qualified Registered Physiotherapists, Physiologists, Psychologists and Rehab Vocational Assessors -- all with a minimum of 7 to 10 years of hands-on experience, ensuring every assessment is thorough, credible, and court-tested.

Our Full Range

Comprehensive Assessment Services

Seven specialist assessment types delivered by experienced, court-tested professionals across Australia.

Earning Capacity Assessments

Comprehensive multi-disciplinary medico-legal earning capacity assessments combining vocational, psychological, and functional evaluations for work capacity decisions and compensation claims.

Learn More

Functional Assessments

Measure a person's level of function and ability to perform functional or work-related tasks safely and reliably. Performed by registered and trained functional assessors using robust testing tools.

Learn More

Workplace Assessments

Examine job duties and physical demands while identifying suitable alternative roles. Crucial in assisting an injured worker's safe return to work with thorough, actionable workplace analysis.

Learn More

Employability Assessments

Identify suitable employment options based on education, training, and experience. Comprehensive evaluation of an individual's employability profile within the current labour market.

Learn More

Vocational Assessments

Identify work options aligned with functional capacity and transferable skills. Thorough analysis of vocational potential to support return-to-work planning and compensation decisions.

Learn More

Labour Market Research

Deep analysis of current labour market conditions and earning potential. Evidence-based research into employment availability, wage rates, and industry trends relevant to individual cases.

Learn More

Pre-Employment Medicals

Baseline health assessments to identify employee suitability for specific roles. Comprehensive screening that helps employers mitigate injury risk and ensure candidates meet job demands safely.
