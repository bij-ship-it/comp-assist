Workplace Assessments Help Your Injured Employees Return to Work

Our workplace assessments examine job duties and physical demands while identifying suitable alternative roles for injured workers. These assessments are crucial in assisting an injured worker's safe return to work, providing the evidence and analysis needed to make informed decisions about workplace accommodations and role modifications.

CAS delivers thorough workplace analysis offering actionable insights to create safer workplaces. Our assessments go beyond simple observation -- we provide detailed, evidence-based recommendations that help employers restructure tasks, reduce risk, and support recovery.

What Our Assessments Cover

Job Duties Analysis

Detailed examination of all job duties and their specific physical, cognitive, and environmental demands

Physical Demands Assessment

Objective measurement and classification of the physical requirements of each role

Alternative Role Identification

Identification of suitable alternative roles that match the worker's current functional capacity

Actionable Recommendations

Practical, evidence-based recommendations for workplace modifications and injury prevention
