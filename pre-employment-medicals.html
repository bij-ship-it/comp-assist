Why Pre-Employment Medicals

Baseline Health Assessments

Pre-employment medical assessments provide a comprehensive baseline health assessment for a worker before entering a role. These assessments assist with risk management and identify medical suitability for specific job requirements.

Conducted by Medical Doctors or specifically trained health professionals, our assessments ensure that prospective employees are physically and medically suited to the demands of their intended role.

Risk Mitigation

Pre-employment medicals allow employers to identify and mitigate risks before placing employees in hazardous or physically demanding roles.

By analyzing a prospective employee's medical history and current health status, businesses can make informed decisions about role suitability and implement appropriate workplace adjustments where necessary.

Reduce Business Downtime & Claims

A thorough pre-employment medical assessment helps reduce the likelihood of insurance claims by ensuring employees are medically fit for their roles from day one.

This proactive approach helps businesses avoid costly circumstances including workplace injuries, extended sick leave, and workers' compensation claims that can significantly impact productivity and profitability.
