About CAS

An Integrated Solutions Provider

Compensation Assistance Services (CAS) and Comp Assist Rehab is an integrated solutions provider with a wealth of proven experience in medicolegal opinions, injury management, return to work services, specialised claims services, and pre-employment medicals.

We provide functional assessments, ADL assessments, vocational assessments, employability assessments, and earning capacity assessments. Our team of expert witness qualified professionals deliver accurate, credible, and concise reports for SIRA/WorkCover, CTP, and Public Liability cases.
